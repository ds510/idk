# Add Your Own Photos

Place your interest photos in this folder and update the `src` in `index.html` for each interest card.

**Suggested filenames:**
- `coding.jpg` or `interest1.jpg`
- `gaming.jpg` or `interest2.jpg`
- `music.jpg` or `interest3.jpg`
- `travel.jpg` or `interest4.jpg`

**Example:** Change `src="https://images.unsplash.com/..."` to `src="images/your-photo.jpg"` in the interest cards.
